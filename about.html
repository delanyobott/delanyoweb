about delanyo agbenyo

I'm an interaction designer who loves solving problems with inclusive design, interface design, and design systems. I love making cool things on the web.

my role

My skills range from fronted and backend development to operations. I design simple interactions for digital services by using exiting or creating new styles, components and patterns.

I help discover user needs and transform them into solutions to meet those needs. I do that by challenging assumptions and prototyping ideas for testing.

I'm more concerned with the way things function as the way they look. My work covers user journeys and navigation as well as micro interactions. I make working and beautiful web software. I'm satisfied when my craft is in the hands of users, delivering values.
